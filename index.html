About Getcontact

Getcontact specializes in caller id and anti-spam measures. The company provides services and products that assist users in identifying incoming calls, managing their contact lists, and safeguarding themselves from unwanted or fraudulent calls.

The main service offered by Getcontact is caller identification. It enables users to view details about incoming calls, such as the name and associated information of the caller, if it is provided. This assists users in making informed decisions regarding whether or not to answer the call.

Getcontact offers spam protection features, along with caller identification. The company's platform utilizes a database of reported numbers to identify and prevent potential nuisance calls, offering users a way to avoid unwanted interactions.

The company's services can be accessed through its mobile application, but the countries where Getcontact operates may differ. The app can be downloaded in various countries.
